Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The values of each crystal will change.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number.
You lose the game if your total score goes about the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Click on a crystal to begin!
